About Me

In 2018 I decided to attend culinary school and pursue my passion of becoming a professional chef. After graduation, I landed my first job at La Caille in Sandy and soon realized that my career choice was rough. Long hours bleeding into 60 a week, with little to no breaks in between, I found myself challenged to stay on this path and left the restaurant. Persistently I continued to work within this industry and found myself in three more kitchens over the next three years. But all the more, nothing changed and my passion was growing dim.
I have always had an interest in computer programming and realized it was time to switch my career path into something more sustainable, practical, and fun. In 2021 I attended Dev Mountain's Web Dev Bootcamp and became hooked with endless possibilities you can accomplish with computers. I enjoy learning about new methods and languages, solving Kata's, and building out applications. The future has become very exciting again and I cant wait to see the growth I will obtain in the years to come.
